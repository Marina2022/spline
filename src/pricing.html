Frequently Asked Questions

  • With Super you are able to organise your scenes in Personal Folders and unlimited editors can join your personal files. You can unlock your creation workflow with a sound library, generating textures with AI, Importing videos and using them as material layers, exporting for iOS AR (USDZ) and exporting for 3D Printing (STL). You are also able to remove the Spline logo on Public URLs and Code Exports.

  • Creating a team is free. You can invite one extra editor and start collaborating right away. To have unlimited team files and editors you’ll need to upgrade to Super Team. You will be billed for each new editor you invite to collaborate.
  • You can cancel your subscription in your account settings by clicking “Cancel Plan”. If you cancel your subscription you will still have access to your Super or Super Team benefits until the end of the active billing period. We do not provide prorated refunds if you cancel after starting a new billing cycle.
  • You can change your subscription recurrence from monthly to yearly in your account settings. You’ll be billed for the yearly price when your current billing period ends.
